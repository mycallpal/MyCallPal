Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You get a new dedicated number that My Call Pal answers. You keep your existing number exactly as it is. Most customers forward their existing number to the My Call Pal number during working hours.
Does it actually sound natural?
Yes. The AI uses advanced voice technology designed to sound human. It handles interruptions, natural pauses, and unexpected answers — not a rigid phone menu.
What happens if I go over my minutes?
Calls continue as normal. You are charged at your tier's overage rate (35p/min on Essential, 30p/min on Professional). You will receive an SMS alert when you reach 80% and 100% of your allowance.
Can it handle emergency calls differently?
Yes. The AI detects emergency language and flags those calls immediately in your SMS with a clear urgent marker. You see it the moment the call ends.
How quickly does the text arrive?
Within 30 seconds of the call ending. By the time you've finished what you're doing, the details are already on your phone.
What if I want to cancel?
Just let us know. No notice period, no cancellation fees. We'd rather you be happy than locked in.
