Questions

Straight answers.

What does my customer actually experience?
They call your number, it's answered promptly in your business name, someone takes their details and reassures them the team will be in touch shortly. It feels like calling a well-run company with a proper reception team. Most callers have no idea it's automated — they just know they were looked after. That matters, because a customer who feels heard is far less likely to call your competitor while they wait.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No. You get a new dedicated number that My Call Pal answers. You keep your existing number exactly as it is. Most customers forward their existing number to the My Call Pal number during working hours.
Does it actually sound natural?
Yes. It's powered by advanced voice technology designed to sound human, not robotic. It handles interruptions, natural pauses, and unexpected answers — not a rigid phone menu.
What happens if I go over my included calls?
Your calls are never cut off. If you go over your included calls, extra calls are simply charged at £1.20 each on Essential or £1.00 each on Professional. You'll never be left without cover — we'd rather bill you a little extra than risk you missing a job.
Can it handle emergency calls differently?
Yes. The AI detects emergency language and flags those calls immediately in your SMS with a clear urgent marker. You see it the moment the call ends.
How quickly does the text arrive?
Within 30 seconds of the call ending. By the time you've finished what you're doing, the details are already on your phone.
What if I want to cancel?
Just let us know. No notice period, no cancellation fees. We'd rather you be happy than locked in.
